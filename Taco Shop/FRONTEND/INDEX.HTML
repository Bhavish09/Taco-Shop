When did tacos first Appear in the United States

Tacos first appeared in the United States in the early 1900s, when Mexican migrants began to bring their food with them while working on railroads and in other jobs. The first mention of tacos in the U.S. was in a newspaper in 1905. (source: Twisted Taco Magazines )
